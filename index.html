How does the buying process work?

The buying process varies by country, but typically includes property selection, offer submission, legal review, signing of contracts, and final payment. We guide you at every step to ensure a secure transaction.

Where can I apply for a mortgage?

ViaEstate partners with international lenders to help you explore your mortgage options, even as a non-resident. Contact us to connect with the right institutions for your needs.

Do I need a home inspection?

An independent inspection evaluates the structure, systems, and safety of the property before purchase. This helps you understand the true condition and avoid unexpected costs.

What documents are required?

You will typically need identification, proof of funds, and signed contracts. We provide clear checklists for every country we operate in.
