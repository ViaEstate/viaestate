About Us

ViaEstate was founded in the summer of 2025 with a bold vision – to transform how real estate is marketed and sold across Europe.

We don’t just provide a platform – we set a new standard. Real estate companies partner with ViaEstate to showcase their properties through a refined, user-focused experience. We are the go-to marketplace for residential properties in Europe, where buyers and sellers connect seamlessly and securely.

We see the gaps in the traditional real estate industry – slow processes, fragmented markets, and lack of accessibility. ViaEstate fills those gaps with innovation, clarity, and speed. We make the property journey smoother for everyone, especially for individuals who don’t know where to begin.

Our ambition drives everything we do. With a strong network across Europe and deep industry insight, we offer end-to-end guidance – from the first thought of buying a home to making it a reality. We constantly evolve, using feedback not just to improve, but to lead.

ViaEstate is not just part of the market – we redefine it. We are your trusted partner in European real estate.
